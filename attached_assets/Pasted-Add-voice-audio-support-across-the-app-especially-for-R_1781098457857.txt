Add voice/audio support across the app, especially for Reading, Writing, and Word Play games.

Main requirement:
For any game that has a question and answer format, add a small speaker/play sound button.

The speaker button should read aloud:

* The question
* The answer options
* Individual words when tapped
* The correct answer after the child answers
* Short encouragement feedback

Use a clear, friendly female English voice.

Reading / Writing / Word Play games:
Add games where children choose the correct word to fill in the blank.

Examples:

3–4 Years:
Question:
“C _ T”
Options:
A, O, B

Audio:

* Speaker reads: “Cat”
* Each option can be tapped to hear the letter sound

5–6 Years:
Question:
“The dog can ___.”
Options:
run, sun, red

Audio:

* Speaker reads: “The dog can blank.”
* Each word option has a speaker button
* Tapping “run” says “run”

7–8 Years:
Question:
“The rabbit quickly ran into the ___.”
Options:
garden, pencil, moon

Audio:

* Speaker reads the full sentence
* Speaker can read each answer option
* After answering, it reads the correct sentence

Add audio to every question-based game:

* Maths
* Reading
* Writing
* Word Play
* Animals
* Science
* Health & Food
* Memory games where useful
* Daily Challenges

Audio requirements:

* Use browser text-to-speech if possible
* Prefer an English female voice
* Keep the voice friendly and clear
* Add a sound on/off toggle in Settings
* Save the sound preference
* Do not autoplay loud sounds
* Only play voice when the child or adult presses the speaker button
* Button click sounds should remain subtle

Review screen audio:
On the review screen, add speaker buttons so adults/children can hear:

* The question
* The child’s answer
* The correct answer
* The explanation

Accessibility:

* Make speaker icons large and easy to press
* Add labels such as “Hear question” and “Hear word”
* Make it useful for children who cannot read confidently yet

Important:
This must work consistently across every game that has questions and answers, not only some reading games.
