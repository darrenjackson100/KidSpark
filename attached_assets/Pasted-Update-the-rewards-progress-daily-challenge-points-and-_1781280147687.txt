Update the rewards, progress, daily challenge, points, and category UI systems.

Rewards approval system:

When a child chooses a reward from the Rewards Shop:

* Do not deduct points immediately
* Create a pending reward request
* Show the child:
  “Reward request sent! Waiting for adult approval.”

Adults must approve or deny every reward request.

Important:
Every approval must require the adult passcode.

Even if the adult has already entered the passcode earlier, approving a reward must always ask for the passcode again.

Approval flow:

1. Child requests reward
2. Request appears in adult area
3. Adult clicks Approve
4. Passcode screen opens
5. Adult enters passcode
6. If correct, points are deducted
7. Reward is marked Approved
8. Reward history is updated

Deny flow:

* Adult can deny the request
* Denying should also require the adult passcode
* Points are not deducted
* Reward history is updated

Children can cancel their own pending reward requests.

Children must not be able to request a reward unless:

* Rewards Shop is enabled
* Reward is active
* Child has enough points at the time of request

Daily Challenge changes:

Daily Challenge should be 20 questions total.

It must be split into 5 questions from each category.

Use 4 categories per daily challenge:

* Maths: 5 questions
* Reading & Words: 5 questions
* Animals: 5 questions
* Health & Food or Science: 5 questions

The child must complete one category before moving to the next.

Example:
Step 1: Maths 1/5 to 5/5
Step 2: Reading 1/5 to 5/5
Step 3: Animals 1/5 to 5/5
Step 4: Health & Food 1/5 to 5/5

Do not allow skipping ahead.

Show progress clearly:

* Current category
* Question number in category
* Overall question number out of 20
* Category progress bar
* Overall progress bar

At the end, show:

* Total score out of 20
* Score by category
* Strongest category
* Needs practice category
* Points earned
* Badges earned

Points balance adjustment:

Points are currently too easy to earn.

Reduce points so low scores give very low rewards.

New suggested points system:

Per correct answer:

* +5 points

Per wrong answer:

* +0 points

Game completion bonus:

* Only give bonus if score is 50% or higher

Score bonuses:

* 0–25% score: +0 bonus
* 26–49% score: +5 bonus
* 50–69% score: +15 bonus
* 70–89% score: +30 bonus
* 90–99% score: +50 bonus
* 100% score: +75 bonus

Daily Challenge:
If child scores:

* 1/20, they should earn almost nothing
* 5/20 or lower, very low points
* 10/20, moderate points
* 15/20+, good points
* 20/20, high points

Example:
1 correct out of 20 = 5 points only

No large bonus should be given for very low scores.

Timed Play:
Points should be based mainly on correct answers and accuracy, not just time spent.

Progress privacy:

Only adults should be able to view progress.

Protect these areas with passcode every time:

* My Progress
* Class page
* Recent Games review
* Child progress details
* Scores
* Charts
* Notes
* Reward history
* Edit child profile

Important:
Each time someone clicks My Progress, it should ask for the passcode.

Do not allow children to open progress by clicking around.

Games, Daily Challenge, Timed Play, sound toggle, and read aloud can stay child accessible.
