Virtual Pet Simplification & Redesign Update

The pet system is becoming too complicated and cluttered. Simplify it and make it feel more natural for children.

Dashboard changes:

Remove these cards completely:

* Points
* Stars
* Badges
* Streak
* Games Played

Replace them with only two large cards/buttons:

1. Rewards Shop

* Shows current points balance
* Example:
  Rewards Shop
  146 Points Available
* Opens the rewards shop
* Children spend points here
* Cleaner and easier to understand

2. My Pet

* Shows pet name
* Shows pet mood
* Shows pet growth stage
* Example:
  My Pet
  Baby Monkey
  Happy 😊
* Opens pet room

This will make the dashboard much cleaner.

---

Pet growth changes

Current problem:

The pet grows far too quickly.

Example:
One game with 10 questions and the pet is already 5/15 progress.

This is far too fast.

New goal:

The pet should grow slowly over weeks, not a few games.

Growth should feel special.

Example growth path:

Stage 1:
Tiny Baby

Stage 2:
Little Baby

Stage 3:

Stage 16:
Adult

Stage 17:
Wise Adult

Stage 18:

Stage 19:
Legendary Friend

Stage 20:
Fully Grown

Make growth take significantly longer.

Suggested:

* Questions answered correctly
* Daily challenges completed
* Reading completed
* Learning streaks

A single game should only provide a very small amount of growth.

The pet should not become fully grown after a few sessions.

Remove Growth tab

Remove:

* Growth tab

Children do not need to see XP numbers.

Children should simply:

* Feed pet
* Play with pet
* Care for pet
* Learn with pet

The pet naturally grows over time.

Keep growth hidden.

---

Remove Style tab

Remove:

* Style tab

Remove:

* Room decorations unlocking onto the pet

Current issue:
Achievements are placing decorations over the pet's face.

Example:
Flower unlock appeared on top of the pet.

This looks broken.

Remove decoration placement entirely for now.

Keep the pet clean and uncluttered.

---

Pet speech bubble

Current issue:
Speech appears high up in the room.

Move the speech bubble so it appears directly above the pet.

Like a real speech bubble.

Requirements:

* Always stay above the pet
* Follow pet position
* Never appear at top of room
* Never cover pet
* Never cover buttons
* Scale correctly as pet grows

When pet grows:

* Bubble should reposition automatically
* Bubble should never sit on the pet's face

---

Pet room simplification

Current pet room should have:

Large pet in centre

Simple room

Food button

Water button

Bath button

Sleep button

Play button

Learn button

Nothing else.

No clutter.

No floating rewards.

No flowers on faces.

No random unlocks inside room.

Keep it clean and polished.

The pet should be the focus.

---

Pet progression philosophy

Children should not think:

"I need XP."

Children should think:

"I want to look after my pet."

Learning should slowly help the pet grow naturally in the background.

Make the system feel magical rather than showing lots of numbers.
